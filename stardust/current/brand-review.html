Tensions decisions for direct

Mechanical detectors per brand-review-template.md § Detectors, run over home-only data.

T-scale

Type scale is ad-hoc

42 → 34 → 22px, ratios 1.235 then 1.545 — no consistent ratio. Direct will need to decide whether the target adopts a modular scale.

Source: _brand-extraction.json § type.scaleAudit
T-cta-vocab

CTA voice fragmented (see-more bucket)

Both "Mehr erfahren" (×3) and "Weiterlesen" (×1) appear for the same read-on affordance. Direct will need to pick a canonical voice for learn-more / read-more.

Source: aggregated pages/home.json § ctas[].label
T-color-imbalance

Emergency red appears as text only

#f43a11 (accent-danger) appears only as text — never as background / border / fill. By design (the 144 emergency label), but Direct must decide: drop, expand to a real semantic-danger token, or keep as a single reserved mark.

Source: _brand-extraction.json § palette[].usedAs
T-logo-variants

Only one logo variant captured

Captured source: img wordmark (188×54 PNG). The redesign will need a monochrome / inverted / SVG variant set (and the wordmark renders 0×0 in the live desktop header due to a responsive swap with the Mediclinic combined-mark icon). Direct should plan that.

Source: _brand-extraction.json § logo
T-img-alt-filename (nuanced)

Filename-style alt text on content images

Several hero/content images carry DAM filenames as alt text ("hirslanden-her-onkologie", "NR_DSC07282", "1455280026", "03_NeueWege-1z1_72dpi"). Not the exact stock strings the mechanical rule catches, but content-free for screen readers. 13% of images have empty alt (below the 30% T-img-alt-empty threshold).

Source: aggregated pages/home.json § media.images[].alt
