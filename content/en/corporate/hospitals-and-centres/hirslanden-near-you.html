Hirslanden near you

The Hirslanden Private Hospital Group is at home all over Switzerland. Our hospitals can be found throughout the entire country, from Lake Constance in the east to Zurich, central Switzerland, Basel and Bern, right through to Lake Geneva in the west.

Each of our hospitals is located in quiet, green surroundings and boasts its own unique style: some offer stunning views of lakes and mountains, some feature world-famous architecture, while others have been making history for decades. Several of our facilities even unite all three of these qualities, which is certainly no small feat. But no matter which hospital you choose, you can look forward to first-class medical treatment, exceptionally qualified doctors and employees, modern infrastructure and a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
