Hospitals and centres

Our emergency departments at a glance

Hirslanden’s emergency centres are staffed around the clock by specially trained interdisciplinary teams of doctors and nurses who provide fast, professional and personalised assistance.

Our emergency centres treat adults and adolescents aged 16 and over, regardless of what type of health insurance they have.

Emergencies involving children

The Hirslanden hospitals do not specialise in paediatric care – except for Clinique des Grangettes. For emergencies involving children, we recommend you go to a children’s hospital or a cantonal hospital.
