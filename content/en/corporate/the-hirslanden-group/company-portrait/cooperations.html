Our cooperations and strategic alliances

In order to offer people integrated, personalised digital, outpatient and inpatient medical care throughout the course of their life in line with the «continuum of care», we establish cooperations with selected public and private partners in areas that go beyond our core business.

The future of sustainable and comprehensive healthcare lies in partnership networks and the expansion of the physical and digital range of services. In cooperation with various partners, we are working on creating an intelligent network of a diverse range of services. In doing so, we work with companies that hold similar values and apply comparable quality standards. We also jointly pursue various projects in the areas of medicine, digitalisation and supplementary insurance. This is essential not only for our patients and partners, but also for an effective, expedient and economical healthcare system.

As part of our Continuum of Care approach we rely on different cooperation models. Examples include the strategic alliance with the Medbase Group in primary care, the equity investment in Spital Lachen and the management agreement with its cardiology department, the cooperation between the different centres of expertise led by partner doctors within a single hospital as part of our system provider model, the Compassana digital healthcare ecosystem managed jointly by the partners Medbase, Groupe Mutuel, Helsana, Swica and Hirslanden as a joint venture or a subsidiary, and the public-private partnership with Hôpitaux Universitaires de Genève (HUG), regulating cooperation between a public hospital and Hirslanden, under which Hirslanden provides the organisational, medical and business expertise.
