Offer for patients with supplementary insurance

Thanks to the cooperation between Medbase and Hirslanden, patients with supplementary insurance benefit from end-to-end coordination and coordinated processes that focus on medical quality along the entire supply chain.

A common point of contact for patients, service providers and insurers enables patient-oriented triage and supports customers on all aspects of their health. It also offers valuable supplementary services through personalised advice and administrative support.
