Our cooperation with See-Spital

Since 2020, the Hirslanden Group and the See-Spital Foundation have been joining forces to further develop medical care for the population of the left bank of Lake Zurich in a way that is close to home, of high quality and sustainable.

The medical services at See-Spital are expanded and deepened by partner doctors from the Hirslanden Group. In contrast, doctors from See-Spital can also perform specialised procedures at Klinik Im Park.

The declared aim of the collaboration is to provide first-class patient care in terms of personnel and infrastructure of great quality in every phase of treatment as well as the use of synergies between the hospital locations.
