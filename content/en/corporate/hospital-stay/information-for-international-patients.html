Information for international patients

Hirslanden International is a specialised team within the Hirslanden Group that focuses exclusively on our international patients. They are your first point of contact for all administrative questions before, during and after your procedure and will organise your stay in consultation with you and your doctor. The team has extensive specialist knowledge and is capable for providing qualified answers to even very complex patient enquiries.

More information you will find on our website in: English, German, French, Russian or Arabic.
